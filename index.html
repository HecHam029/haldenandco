06 · QUESTIONS

Asked first.

What does Halden & Co. actually do?

Three things, run together: strategy, governance and compliance. In practice that means privacy programmes, contracts, AI governance frameworks, audits and corporate advisory, built around how your business actually operates.

Do you only handle data protection?

No. Data protection anchors the practice, it does not limit it. We draft and review contracts, build internal policies, set up AI governance and advise on regulatory questions across sectors.

Can you draft and review contracts?

Yes. NDAs, DPAs, vendor and service agreements, employment documents and terms. Everything is drafted to work with your compliance posture rather than against it.

Does the DPDP Act apply to my business?

If you handle the personal data of people in India, almost certainly yes. The threshold is low and the exemptions are limited. A short review tells you exactly where you stand, and GDPR is layered in where you serve users abroad.

How long does an engagement take?

Most early-stage programmes run in weeks rather than months. We confirm a realistic timeline after the first assessment, before you commit to anything.

Is this confidential?

Every conversation is handled in strict confidence from the first call. Your details are used only to respond to your enquiry.
